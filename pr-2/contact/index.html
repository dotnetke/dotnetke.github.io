Contact Us

Get in touch with the .NET Kenya Community! We're always excited to connect with fellow developers, potential speakers, and anyone interested in .NET technologies.

How to Reach Us​

Social Media​

  • Twitter: @dotnetke
  • LinkedIn: .NET Kenya Community
  • GitHub: github.com/dotnetke

Community Platforms​

  • Meetup: .NET Kenya Community
  • Email: hello@dotnetkenya.org (coming soon)

Get Involved​

🎤 Become a Speaker​

Have knowledge to share? We're always looking for speakers to present at our events!

What we look for:

  • .NET-related topics (any level)
  • Real-world experiences and case studies
  • Open source projects and contributions
  • Career advice and industry insights

How to apply:

  1. Reach out via our social media channels
  2. Submit a brief proposal with your topic
  3. Include a short bio and any relevant links

🤝 Volunteer with Us​

Help us grow the community! We need volunteers for:

  • Event organization and coordination
  • Content creation and blog writing
  • Social media management
  • Mentoring new developers

💻 Contribute to Projects​

Check out our GitHub organization for:

  • Community website improvements
  • Sample projects and tutorials
  • Learning resources and documentation

Community Guidelines​

Our community is built on:

  • Inclusivity - Everyone is welcome regardless of experience level
  • Learning - We're all here to grow and share knowledge
  • Respect - We treat everyone with kindness and respect
  • Collaboration - We believe in building together

We follow the .NET Foundation Code of Conduct.

Office Hours​

While we don't have a physical office, our community organizers are typically available:

  • Twitter DMs: Daily responses
  • GitHub Issues: 24-48 hour response time
  • Meetup Messages: Weekly check-ins

Frequently Asked Questions​

Q: Do I need to be an expert to join? A: Not at all! We welcome developers of all skill levels.

Q: Are events free? A: Yes, all our community events are completely free.

Q: Can I suggest event topics? A: Absolutely! We love hearing what the community wants to learn about.

Q: How can I sponsor an event? A: Great question! Reach out to us via any of our channels to discuss sponsorship opportunities.

Ready to join our growing community? We can't wait to meet you! 🚀
