# Welcome to .NET Kenya Community!

We're excited to launch the official .NET Kenya community website! This platform will serve as a central hub for all .NET developers in Kenya and those interested in learning about Microsoft's .NET ecosystem.

- Regular meetups and workshops

- Job board and career resources

- .NET news and announcements
- Local tech events and conferences
- Success stories from our community members
