## Event Guidelines

- All events are free and open to everyone
- We follow a [Code of Conduct](https://dotnetfoundation.org/about/code-of-conduct) to ensure a welcoming environment
- Events are conducted in English and Swahili when appropriate
- Both in-person and virtual events are organized
