# Welcome to .NET Kenya Community! 🇰🇪

We're thrilled to launch the official website for the .NET Kenya Community! This marks a significant milestone in our journey to build a vibrant and supportive ecosystem for .NET developers across Kenya and East Africa.

<!--truncate-->

## Our Mission

The .NET Kenya Community exists to:

- **Connect** developers passionate about .NET technologies
- **Share** knowledge through meetups, workshops, and online resources  
- **Support** career growth and professional development
- **Collaborate** on open source projects and community initiatives
- **Celebrate** the achievements of our members

## What You'll Find Here

Our new website serves as your central hub for everything .NET Kenya:

### 🗓️ [Events](/events)
Stay updated on our regular meetups, workshops, and special events. Each event page includes speaker information, presentation materials, and recordings when available.

### 📚 [Courses](/courses/intro) 
Access our growing library of courses designed specifically for Kenyan developers. From .NET fundamentals to advanced topics, all with local context and real-world examples.

### ✍️ [Blog](/blog)
Read community news, technical articles, career advice, and insights from fellow developers in the Kenyan tech ecosystem.

### 📞 [Contact](/contact)
Learn how to get involved, become a speaker, volunteer, or just connect with the community.

## Join Our Growing Community

Whether you're a seasoned .NET architect or just starting your programming journey, there's a place for you in our community. We believe in:

- **Inclusive learning** - Everyone starts somewhere, and we're here to support each other
- **Knowledge sharing** - Your experiences, however small, can help others grow
- **Professional growth** - Connect with mentors, find job opportunities, and advance your career
- **Fun and friendship** - Technology is better when shared with great people!
